⚠️ Disclaimers & Important Information

Paper Trading Only

Alex is not placing actual trades. This page documents signals Alex would have taken. No contract sizing is calculated — risk is set to a fixed 0.25% of account balance per trade, compounding daily. Alex trades a lower win rate, higher risk-to-reward strategy with tight stop losses. Alex may not find a valid entry every day. Trade tracking began December 8th, 2025.

Algorithm Development

Past profits/losses may be misrepresented due to active algorithm development and updates. The trading strategy is continuously being refined, and significant updates that alter trading behavior are documented in the Updates section. Historical performance should be viewed with this context in mind.
